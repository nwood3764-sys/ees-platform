Occupancy

50121 files: built from a known-good 50121 export so they import + submit. Live-mapped from your Asset Score report: name, address, year, gross & use-type floor areas, window-to-wall ratio, wall area, conditioned floors, envelope R-values, boiler (capacity/efficiency/year), water heater, headline EUIs, and the 50121 whole-building savings %. Occupancy is computed from the units & bedrooms above (2 in the master + 1 per additional bedroom). Monthly energy loads from the utility spreadsheet (DOE loads that separately). HVAC delivery/measures detail is the remaining refinement pass.
